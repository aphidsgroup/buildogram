# BUILDOGRAM FULL ECOSYSTEM MASTER PRD

# Purpose

This document defines Buildogram as a full ecosystem product from Day 1.

It is not an MVP product document.

It is the master PRD for launching Buildogram as a complete property ownership operating system with:

- Construction
- BOQ transparency
- Quality verification
- Material marketplace
- Partner ecosystem
- Property Passport
- 360 rental platform
- 360 resale platform
- Maintenance
- AI tools
- Content engine
- Social media showcase engine
- IT and promotion services for partners

Core statement:

**Buildogram is not a construction company. Buildogram is a property lifecycle platform.**

---

# Product Vision

Buildogram should become the trusted digital layer for property ownership.

A customer should be able to use Buildogram to:

1. Understand what to build
2. Estimate cost
3. Review plans
4. Audit BOQ
5. Build with verified execution
6. Track progress
7. Verify quality
8. Buy materials at market-best rates
9. Store all proof in Property Passport
10. Rent property with 360 tours
11. Sell property with verified records
12. Maintain property after handover
13. Work with trusted builders, contractors, architects and suppliers

---

# Core Brand Promise

Buildogram gives property owners:

- Clarity before decision

# Success Criteria

Buildogram full ecosystem launch is successful when:

- Users understand the complete ecosystem
- Leads come from multiple verticals

# Final Product Principle

Buildogram should not be built as a small first version.

It should be built as a full ecosystem where some parts are automated and some parts are manually fulfilled in the beginning.

The platform must communicate the complete future from Day 1.

Final statement:

**Buildogram is the operating system for property ownership — from planning and construction to materials, verification, rental, resale and maintenance.**
