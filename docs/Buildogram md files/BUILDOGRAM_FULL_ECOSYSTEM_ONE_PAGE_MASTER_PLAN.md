# BUILDOGRAM FULL ECOSYSTEM ONE-PAGE MASTER PLAN

# Buildogram

## India's Property Transparency Platform

**Build with proof, not promises.**

Buildogram is a full property lifecycle platform that helps people plan, build, verify, buy materials, rent, sell and maintain property with proof.

It is not just a construction company.

It is a connected ecosystem for property owners, builders, contractors, architects, suppliers, tenants, buyers and maintenance vendors.

---

# Core Vision

Property decisions in India are expensive but unclear.

Customers face:

- Confusing construction cost
- Vague contractor quotations
- Material doubts
- Quality issues
- Delays
- Scattered documents
- Poor rental/resale listing trust
- No maintenance history

Buildogram solves this by creating a proof-based property operating system.

# Main Moat

## Property Passport™

Property Passport is the permanent digital proof record of a property.

It stores:

- Legal documents
- Drawings
- BOQ
- Material records
- Quality checks
- Progress photos/videos
- 360 tours
- Warranty
- Maintenance history
- Rental readiness
- Resale readiness

This makes the property easier to maintain, rent, sell and verify.
