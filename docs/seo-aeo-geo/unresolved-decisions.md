# Unresolved Decisions (Legal & SEO/AEO/GEO)

## 1. Indemnity and Hold-Harmless Clause

**Status:** Pending solicitor drafting and approval — do not represent as final.

**Context:** We need a properly drafted indemnity and hold-harmless clause that covers Buildogram for claims arising from third-party (contractor/supplier) breaches, but *without* unlawfully excluding Buildogram's own liability for its expressly contracted advisory/coordination services or professional negligence.

**Requirements for Solicitor:**
Please draft a clause covering (where legally enforceable):
- Claims arising from a contractor’s workmanship, labour, site safety, statutory violations or execution.
- Contractor breach of warranty.
- Supplier or manufacturer defects.
- Inaccurate information or documents provided by the client or partner.
- Unauthorised client or contractor changes made contrary to reviewed drawings or instructions.
- Intellectual-property or regulatory violations committed by third parties.

**Constraint:** The clause must *not* attempt to indemnify Buildogram for Buildogram’s own fraud, wilful misconduct or obligations that cannot legally be excluded.
