# Offline Contract Alignment Checklist

This checklist is for the legal team to ensure that all physical/PDF contracts signed offline align perfectly with Buildogram's platform positioning as an **Advisory, Construction Intelligence, Coordination, and Property Assurance Consultant**.

DO NOT GENERATE A SUPPOSEDLY FINAL CLIENT CONTRACT WITHOUT SOLICITOR APPROVAL.

## 1. Buildogram Advisory and Coordination Agreement
This is the agreement between the Client and Buildogram. It MUST govern:
- [ ] Buildogram’s professional scope
- [ ] Fees and payment flow for Buildogram's services
- [ ] Deliverables (e.g., reports, property passport)
- [ ] Exclusions (e.g., execution, direct warranty, daily site safety supervision)
- [ ] Limitations of liability and indemnity
- [ ] Communication, reporting, and documentation protocols

## 2. Contractor Execution Agreement
This is the agreement between the Client and the Appointed Builder/Contractor. It MUST govern:
- [ ] Construction execution and methods
- [ ] Full scope of work and specifications
- [ ] Final BOQ and Price
- [ ] Payment schedule (paid to contractor, not Buildogram)
- [ ] Variation process
- [ ] Workmanship and labour control
- [ ] Site safety (Contractor's responsibility)
- [ ] Project programme and delay penalties
- [ ] Defects, rectification, and retention
- [ ] Structural and workmanship warranties (issued by Contractor)
- [ ] Termination and dispute resolution

## 3. Other Legal Documents to Align
- [ ] Consultant appointments (e.g., architects, structural engineers)
- [ ] Supplier orders
- [ ] Scope matrix (Clearly defining who does what)
- [ ] Responsibility matrix (e.g., distinguishing Buildogram's visual checks vs. Contractor's execution)
- [ ] BOQ and specification annexures
- [ ] Warranty documents
- [ ] Insurance requirements
- [ ] Governing law, jurisdiction, and force majeure
- [ ] Data and Property Passport records ownership

## 4. India & Tamil Nadu Specific Legal Review Items
The solicitor must review applicability and drafting under:
- [ ] Indian Contract Act, 1872
- [ ] Consumer Protection Act, 2019
- [ ] Applicable e-commerce and advertising requirements
- [ ] Information Technology Act and relevant digital-platform rules
- [ ] Tamil Nadu-specific contract and construction requirements
- [ ] RERA applicability (if Buildogram’s role or any project falls within promoter or real-estate-agent definitions) - *Note: Do not state RERA definitely applies or doesn't apply; flag for project-model-specific advice.*
- [ ] Professional-negligence exposure
- [ ] Tax and payment-flow implications
- [ ] Arbitration, courts, and jurisdiction
- [ ] Insurance requirements
