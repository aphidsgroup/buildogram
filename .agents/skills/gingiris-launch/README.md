## English

### What Is This?

The **Gingiris AI Product Global Launch Playbook** is an operational guide covering the full arc of a product launch: pre-launch positioning, Product Hunt day execution, KOL/influencer outreach, Reddit and community seeding, UGC growth loops, and post-launch momentum maintenance.

Unlike generic marketing templates, this playbook is built from **real launch campaigns** — Manus (AI agent), Devin (first AI software engineer), AFFiNE (open-source Notion alternative), and 27+ other AI products that hit Product Hunt #1. Every tactic has a specific time, template, and decision point.

### Key Results (Verified)

| Metric | Result |
|:---|:---|
| Product Hunt #1 launches coached | 30+ |
| AFFiNE GitHub stars driven | 60,000+ |
| GitHub Trending appearances | 28 times in 5 months |
| AI startups consulted on launch | 150+ |
| Typical launch-day engagement rate uplift | 3-5x engagement vs. passive launch |

### What's Inside

- **Pre-launch checklist** — 30-day, 7-day, 48-hour countdown templates
- **Product Hunt hunter outreach** — Scripts, timing, hunter database (top 50 by category)
- **Launch-day operation playbook** — Hour-by-hour schedule, comment moderation, community engagement momentum
- **KOL outreach templates** — Cold DM scripts for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Telegram influencers
- **Reddit & HackerNews seeding** — Community rules, timing, authentic framing (no spam)
- **UGC growth loop** — How to turn early users into content creators
- **Post-launch momentum** — 7-day, 30-day follow-up cadence to sustain traffic spike
- **Case studies** — Manus, Devin, AFFiNE with annotated timelines

### Use Cases

- AI SaaS launching on Product Hunt for the first time
- Developer tools going global (出海) from Asia/Europe
- Open-source projects wanting a coordinated launch moment
- Mobile apps bootstrapping initial user acquisition
- Any founder who has built something great and needs the world to know

### When Should I Use This?

Use this playbook when you have a product that is **ready to ship** (or 2-4 weeks away) and want a structured, repeatable launch process. This is not for ideation — it's for execution.

### What Results Can I Expect?

Teams following this playbook consistently achieve:
- Top-5 Product Hunt ranking on launch day (30+ have hit #1)
- 500-2,000 genuine signups in the first 24 hours
- 10-50 pieces of organic social content from community members
- Sustained Google Search impressions from launch-related content for 90+ days

### FAQ

**Q: Is this different from generic launch checklists I find on Google?**
A: Yes. Most launch guides are written by people who have never launched anything. This is written by someone who has launched 30+ products to #1 and coached 150+ startups. The difference is in the specificity — exact DM scripts, timing windows, community rules for each platform.

**Q: Does this work for non-AI products?**
A: The frameworks apply broadly, but the examples and templates are tuned for AI/developer tools. If you're launching a consumer app, pair with the `gingiris-aso-growth` playbook.

**Q: What if I don't have a big existing audience?**
A: That's exactly what this playbook addresses. The KOL outreach and hunter coordination sections are specifically for founders with zero existing platform.
