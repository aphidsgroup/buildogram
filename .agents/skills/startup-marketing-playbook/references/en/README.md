# Startup Marketing on $0 Budget — Organic Channels Only

No money? No problem. Every tactic here costs $0 — just your time and creativity.

## SEO from Scratch

- How to rank without backlinks or domain authority
- Zero-budget SEO is a time investment, not a money investment — start before you think you're ready

## Community Marketing, Not Community Spamming

- Provide genuine value inside existing communities instead of broadcasting your product
- The line that matters: contribute first, mention your product only where it actually answers someone's problem
- Value-first participation is what keeps you welcome — spam gets you removed and burns the channel

## Social Media Playbook

- Platform-specific content that drives signups organically
- Don't post the same thing everywhere: each platform rewards a different content shape

## Word-of-Mouth Engineering

- Make the product itself naturally shareable, so users do the marketing for you
- Word-of-mouth is designed into the product experience, not bolted on afterwards

## Guerrilla Tactics

- Creative, scrappy approaches that punch above their weight on $0
- Constraints breed creativity: the lack of budget forces sharper positioning and better ideas

## B2B vs. 2C Calibration

This playbook defaults to dev tools / open source / B2B SaaS. If your product is 2C (consumer, education, apps, games):

- Swap core metrics to D1/D7/D30 retention, activation rate, and viral coefficient K — not MRR/CAC/LTV
- Swap cold-start channels to vertical communities, short video, and niche KOLs — not Product Hunt / Hacker News / LinkedIn
- Full 2C adaptation guide with per-country channel data lives in `gingiris-seo-geo/references/2c-adaptation.md`
