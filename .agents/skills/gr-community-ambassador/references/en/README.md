# Community & Ambassador Program Playbook — Full SOP

From pre-launch checklist to recruitment, tiered management, retention, and governance. Built from Notion (20M users, real ambassador interviews), AFFiNE (60K stars), Asana Ambassador, and ClickUp Verified.

## Core Premise

- **Goal is mutual achievement**: users build personal influence and a monetization path; the product gets community-driven global expansion — a user–content–product flywheel
- **What ambassadors really are**: not badge-wearing promoters — the real draws are the chance to become a KOL, direct dialogue with founders, and a path to monetization
- **Operating philosophy**: long-term companionship and shared growth, never one-off traffic harvesting

## Before You Launch — All Six Must Be True

- Whole team uses your own product, and it is embedded in daily workflows
- Product has reached a genuinely "usable" state
- You have content capacity, a shareable roadmap, and a dedicated owner
- **Stage fit**: pre-PMF — don't start, find 100 deep users first; growth stage — 5–10 seed ambassadors with no quotas; scale stage — formal program with points; mature — full ecosystem plus affiliate

## Recruitment & Tiers

- **Entry bar (A and B together)**: A — can organize events; B — can produce content (video, writing, or templates)
- Quality over quantity: Notion's roster runs to hundreds of names, but only ~25% are active
- **Progression**: Bronze (on application) → Silver (≥100 pts/yr, beta priority + official retweets) → Gold (≥300, 1:1 contact + annual event) → Platinum (≥600, custom support + sponsorship)
- Points examples: tutorial long-form 20, video 30, local event 50, bug report 10, doc translation 15, accepted community answer 3

## Five Incentive Pillars (Notion insights)

1. The product is actually good — the precondition for everything else
2. Beta-first access — the "insider" feeling
3. Direct founder dialogue — the strongest incentive of all
4. Help them become a KOL — official retweets and announcements
5. Retweet every creator — incentivize everyone who mentions you

## Churn Early Warning

- Two months without contribution → personal check-in DM
- Quality declining → offer training resources; feeling unseen → 1:1 plus amplification

## Health Benchmarks

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Member growth | +15%/month |
| WAU | ≥30% |
| Weekly UGC | ≥10 pieces |
| Support response | ≤24h |
| Ambassador active rate | ≥25% |
| Ambassador churn | ≤20%/year |
