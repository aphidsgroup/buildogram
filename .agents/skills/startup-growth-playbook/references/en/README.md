# Startup Growth Playbook — Seed-Stage Channel Selection

You have PMF but limited resources. This playbook helps you pick the ONE channel that will work — and invest where traction compounds.

## Channel Scoring Matrix

- Evaluate 15+ growth channels against your specific product and audience
- Score channels before spending on them: a channel that works for someone else's product is not evidence it fits yours
- The output is a ranked shortlist, not a to-do list — seed-stage teams cannot run five channels at once

## PMF-to-Growth Bridge

- Know the signals that say you're ready to scale vs. still iterating on the product
- Scaling acquisition before those signals show up wastes your limited runway
- Growth work and PMF work are different modes — be honest about which one you're in

## Organic vs. Paid

- A decision framework based on three inputs: CAC, LTV, and runway
- Don't pick paid or organic by taste — let the unit economics and your remaining runway decide

## Founder-Channel Fit

- Some channels work for you specifically because of who the founder is
- Match the channel to the founder's existing strengths and audience instead of copying another company's motion

## Sequencing Strategy

- Which channel first, second, third — and the signals that tell you when to switch
- Sequence deliberately: prove one channel before layering the next

## B2B vs. 2C Calibration

This playbook defaults to dev tools / open source / B2B SaaS. If your product is 2C (consumer, education, apps, games):

- Swap core metrics to D1/D7/D30 retention, activation rate, and viral coefficient K — not MRR/CAC/LTV
- Swap cold-start channels to vertical communities, short video, and niche KOLs — not Product Hunt / Hacker News / LinkedIn
- Full 2C adaptation guide with per-country channel data lives in `gingiris-seo-geo/references/2c-adaptation.md`
